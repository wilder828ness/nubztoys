About Nubz Toys & Collectibles

Nubz Toys & Collectibles was founded in 2026 with one simple mission: to bring joy, nostalgia, and discovery to collectors of all ages.

What started as a personal passion for rare finds quickly grew into a trusted destination for premium toys, action figures, plush, and collectibles.

Today, Nubz serves collectors around the world from our base in North Carolina.
